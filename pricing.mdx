## Pricing Details

### What Counts as a Billable Event?

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Identify API" icon="fingerprint">
    Only **successful authentications** are charged. Failed attempts, user cancellations, and errors are free.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Sign API" icon="file-signature">
    Each **completed signature**, regardless of document size. Declined or expired requests are not charged.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Message API" icon="message-check">
    Each **message sent**, with different rates for text-only vs. messages with attachments.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How is billing calculated?" icon="calculator">
    You're only charged for successful API calls. Failed requests are not billed.

    - **Identify API:** Only successful authentications
    - **Sign API:** Only completed signatures
    - **Message API:** Only messages successfully delivered
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do I get any credits to start?" icon="gift">
    Yes! When you join UIP, you get $5 in credits to try out all APIs. Perfect for testing and initial implementation.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="When am I billed?" icon="calendar">
    Billing is monthly based on your usage. You'll receive an invoice at the end of each month with detailed usage statistics. Pay only for what you use at standard rates.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What payment methods do you accept?" icon="credit-card">
    We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express). Payment is processed automatically at the end of each billing cycle.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I track my usage?" icon="chart-line">
    Your dashboard provides real-time usage analytics, cost breakdowns, and usage forecasts to help you manage your spending.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I set spending limits?" icon="gauge">
    Yes! You can set monthly spending limits in your dashboard. When you approach your limit, we'll send email notifications. You can choose to increase your limit or pause new requests.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do audit queries cost extra?" icon="magnifying-glass-dollar">
    No! All audit trail queries are completely free and unlimited. Store reference IDs forever and query them anytime at no additional cost.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if I exceed my free tier?" icon="arrow-trend-up">
    Your account automatically switches to paid usage. We'll notify you when you're approaching the 1,000-call limit. After exceeding it, standard rates apply automatically—no manual upgrade needed.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

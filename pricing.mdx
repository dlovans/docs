description: "Usage-based, per-successful-step. No subscription."
---

Alentra is **pay-as-you-go**. You're charged per **successfully verified step** — never
for sessions you create, abandoned flows, or steps that fail verification. The proof
is the product; if a step doesn't produce a verified result, it isn't billed.

## Per-primitive prices

| Primitive | Price | Wallet units | What it verifies |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `identify` | **$0.10** | 1,000 | Government-attested identity fields you request |
| `age_verify` | **$0.05** | 500 | An age-threshold boolean; identity withheld from the business |
| `light_sign` | **$0.15** | 1,500 | A device signature over inline terms text |
| `sign` | **$0.30** | 3,000 | A device signature over one or more documents |

<Note>
  A multi-step session is billed per step: an `identify` + `sign` session that
  completes both steps costs $0.10 + $0.30 = **$0.40**. If the user stops after the
  first step, you're billed only **$0.10**.
</Note>

## How billing works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Top up your balance">
    Add at least **$5** manually from Billing through embedded Stripe Checkout.
    Applicable Stripe Tax is added above the selected wallet credit, never deducted
    from it. The payment and its Stripe identifiers are recorded in the billing ledger.
    Your balance is held in **wallet units** — `1 USD = 10,000` units.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Spend per verified step">
    Each step that verifies successfully deducts its price from your balance, charged
    in the same atomic commit as the result and audit record. A failed verification
    charges nothing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Stay within your floor">
    Self-serve accounts can dip into a small overdraft buffer so a momentary low
    balance does not immediately block a paying user. Once the buffer is exhausted,
    Alentra refuses additional paid progress; top up promptly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Am I charged when a user abandons a session?">
    No. Creating a session is free, and only steps that produce a verified result are
    billed. An abandoned or expired session costs nothing.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is there a subscription or minimum?">
    No subscription, no monthly minimum. You pay only for verified steps.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do prices differ by credential route?">
    No. Pricing is per primitive, not per country, credential, or wallet provider —
    an `identify` step costs $0.10 on every available route.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What happens after a refund or dispute?">
    An actionable Stripe refund or dispute freezes new customer API activity while
    Alentra staff reconcile the corresponding wallet credit. Reconciliation never alters
    completed session results, original signed documents, or audit evidence.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

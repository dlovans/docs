UIP is **pay-as-you-go**. You're charged per **successfully verified step** — never
for sessions you create, abandoned flows, or steps that fail verification. The proof
is the product; if a step doesn't produce a verified result, it isn't billed.

## Per-primitive prices

| Primitive | Price | What it verifies |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `identify` | **$0.01** | Government-attested identity fields you request |
| `age_verify` | **$0.01** | An anonymous age-threshold boolean |
| `light_sign` | **$0.02** | A device signature over inline terms text |
| `sign` | **$0.03** | A device signature over one or more documents |

<Note>
  A multi-step session is billed per step: an `identify` + `sign` session that
  completes both steps costs $0.01 + $0.03 = **$0.04**. If the user stops after the
  first step, you're billed only **$0.01**.
</Note>

## How billing works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Top up your balance">
    Add funds to your business wallet from the dashboard (card payment). Your balance
    is held in **wallet units** — `1 USD = 10,000` units — so sub-cent prices are
    exact.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Spend per verified step">
    Each step that verifies successfully deducts its price from your balance, charged
    the moment the result is durably recorded. A failed verification charges nothing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Stay within your floor">
    Self-serve accounts can dip into a small overdraft buffer so a momentary low
    balance never blocks a paying user; a session is cut off once you cross the floor.
    Top up to resume.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Am I charged when a user abandons a session?">
    No. Creating a session is free, and only steps that produce a verified result are
    billed. An abandoned or expired session costs nothing.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is there a subscription or minimum?">
    No subscription, no monthly minimum. You pay only for verified steps.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do prices differ by wallet or country?">
    Pricing is per primitive, not per wallet. As new wallets and countries come
    online, the same per-primitive prices apply.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
